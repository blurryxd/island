I wake up in a motorboat. There is water surrounding you everywhere but north. There is also a note right next to you... There's something mysterious about this isle, but I am not sure what it is. I should probably leave, but the boat seems to be out of gas. The note is warning me about the Island, but I need to find a way the get more gasoline. Should I go explore the area? The foggy island looks creepy and abandoned. Where is everyone? Would be foolish to go explore the unknown, but there is no other way. As I leave the docks I feel like someone, or something, is watching me. But I can’t see anybody. This place feels haunted….


The Island is a text-adventure game based off on un-defined era in between 1800's into 2000's. You need to explore the area and solve puzzles to get out of the island and win the game! The Island is small area with several buildings and places to explore. What to do next? It’s up to you!

Commads you can use:

Movement: north, west, east and south.

Interaction with the world: use, read, pickup, open, examine.
